# Light Step  *Feat 6*  
[rogue](../../Rules/traits/rogue.md)  


You aren't bothered by tricky footing. When you [Stride](../../Rules/actions/stride.md) or [Step](../../Rules/actions/step.md), you can ignore difficult terrain.

*Source: Core Rulebook p. 186*
